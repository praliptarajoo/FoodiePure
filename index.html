About Us

Come join us in GoGreen challenge......

Eat what you should and grow what you want to eat.

Our objective behind starting this concept is giving all the consumers and farmers a common platform. Here you can find a perfect farmer for your uncultivated land who will plant what you want to eat in an organic way.

Our soul motive is to avoid adultration and contamination of green vegetables which has become a concern world wide. This will not only give employment to all those farmers who do not have a land but also a scope to all the land owners to properly utilize their unused lands.
